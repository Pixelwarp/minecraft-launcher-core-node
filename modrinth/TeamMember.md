### permissions

```ts
permissions: number
```
The user's permissions in bitfield format (requires authorization to view)

In order from first to eighth bit, the bits are:

- UPLOAD_VERSION
- DELETE_VERSION
- EDIT_DETAILS
- EDIT_BODY
- MANAGE_INVITES
- REMOVE_MEMBER
- EDIT_MEMBER
- DELETE_PROJECT
